About Me

Colin M. Williams is a dynamic team player with instincts like a ninja. He is a North Carolina native with a love for Chicago, Colorado and California. Colin has a drive for solving problems and developing collaborative business relationships. With a young love for Lego it is fascinating, fun and stimulating for Colin to apply the skills learned from building blocks to our current state of technology.

As an avid fan of the Carolina Panthers and the blue team of Durham you may hear a sports analogy while discussing the current project or a reference to a song lyric, movie line, or video game. Beyond people, problem-solving and the fantasy world of Marvel and Star Wars it is important to mention Colin has a passion for food, good food. Having spent the past 10 years in the hospitality industry, he is highly professional when it comes to customer service, promoting a specific brand, and delicious food. He enjoys spending time with his wonderful friends and amazing family when he is not fully focused on making the world a better place through coding.
